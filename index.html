Z-825 Model — Structured for resilience

A 4-year capital cycle designed for predictable pooling, disciplined governance, and scalable deployment.

Contribution Rules

  • KSh 825 standard monthly contribution.
  • Due each month by the 10th.
  • KSh 100 penalty per week late.
  • Accelerated contribution path available.

Governance Guardrails

  • Written proposals required for project funding.
  • 70% member agreement to approve disbursements.
  • Rotational operations to reduce founder dependency.
  • Early exits carry a capital forfeiture clause.

Capital Protection

  • 5% of profits allocated to CPF.
  • Emergency liquidity and downside cushioning.
  • Internal stability before external expansion.
  • Risk review embedded in every phase.

Operational Roadmap

Execution is staged to preserve momentum while improving decision quality over time.

Phase 1 — Building

Team education, policy alignment, and formal business registration.

Phase 2 — Growth

Capital cycle activation, internal lending, project seeding, and investor readiness.

Phase 3 — Scale

Institutional formalization and launch of a dedicated management technology platform.
