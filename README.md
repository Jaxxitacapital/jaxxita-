# Jaxxita Enterprises — Strategic Framework

Jaxxita Enterprises is the umbrella entity, with **Jaxxita Capital** operating as the investment and micro-lending arm focused on youth-led financial inclusion in Africa.

Build sustainable capital cycles and foster financial inclusion for young innovators across Africa.

## Z-825 Investment Model (Sept 2025 → Sept 2029)

### Contribution Framework
- **Monthly contribution:** KSh 825 per member
- **Due date:** On or before the 10th of each month
- **Late penalty:** KSh 100 per week
- **Acceleration option:** KSh 1,650 per month earns 2.5 shares (instead of 1)

### Equity & Ownership
- **Founder stake:** 70% base capital stake with preferred dividend rights
- **Participating shareholders:** Ian, David, Dennis (initial set)
- **Share calculation basis:** Duration + consistency of KSh 825 participation

---

## Governance & Risk Management

### Capital Protection Strategies
1. **5% Rule (CPF):** 5% of annual profits allocated to the Capital Preservation Fund
2. **Vetting threshold:** Written proposal + 70% member approval required for borrowing/funding
3. **Rotation policy:** Rotating operational roles to avoid founder dependency and improve leadership depth
4. **Exit clause:** Early exit before Sept 2029 incurs 10–15% capital forfeiture

---

## Operational Roadmap
- **Phase 1 — Building:** Team education + formal registration
- **Phase 2 — Growth:** Capital cycle execution + internal lending + project seeding
- **Phase 3 — Scale:** Full company formalization + dedicated management tech platform

---

## Legal & IP
- **Jurisdiction:** Kenyan law
- **IP split:** 50/50 between project founder and Jaxxita (for Jaxxita-funded creations)
- **Confidentiality:** Strict restrictions on financial and project information with penalties for breaches
